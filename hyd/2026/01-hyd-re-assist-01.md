# HYD Real Estate Assistant 01
For a budget of ₹90L (±15%) with a focus on family living and future growth in 2026–2027, the best residential strategy in Hyderabad involves targeting "outskirts-plus" zones. These are areas bordering the Nehru Outer Ring Road (ORR) that are transitioning from emerging to established residential corridors.

## Top Recommended Areas for 2026–2027

### 1. Bachupally / Bowrampet (North-West)

* **Distance to Airport**: ~43–48 KM via Nehru ORR.
* **Suitability**: Best for Family Living. High density of quality schools and healthcare.
* **Price Range**: 3BHK: ₹75L – ₹1.1 Cr; Plots: ₹45k – ₹65k/sq.yd.
* **Infrastructure**: Connects via ORR Exit 4. Proximity to Miyapur Metro (Phase 1) and upcoming Phase 2.
* **Downsides**: Growing traffic congestion on internal arterial roads.
* **Choice**: Apartments for families; high-end gated communities like Urbanrise On Cloud33 are prominent. 

### 2. Tellapur / Kollur (Western Corridor)

* **Distance to Airport**: ~30–40 KM via ORR Exit 2.
* **Suitability**: Balanced (Lifestyle + Appreciation). The premier choice for IT professionals.
* **Price Range**: 3BHK: ₹85L – ₹1.4 Cr; Plots: ₹80k – ₹1.2L/sq.yd.
* **Infrastructure**: 10-15 mins from Financial District and Neopolis. Direct ORR access.
* **Downsides**: Internal infrastructure like sewage and local roads is still catching up to the high-rise boom.
* **Choice**: Apartments for modern lifestyle amenities; Plots for long-term luxury villas.  

### 3. Kompally / Gundlapochampally (North)

* **Distance to Airport**: ~50–55 KM via NH-44 and ORR.
* **Suitability**: Best for Future Appreciation. Known for its greenery and peaceful vibe.
* **Price Range**: 3BHK: ₹65L – ₹95L; Plots: ₹40k – ₹55k/sq.yd.
* **Infrastructure**: NH-44 expansion and proximity to the upcoming Kandlakoya IT Park.
* **Downsides**: Limited public transit; highly dependent on private vehicles.
* **Choice**: Plots are high-growth assets here; apartments for those seeking budget-friendly gated communities. 

### 4. Adibatla / Tukkuguda (South)

* **Distance to Airport**: ~11–15 KM.
* **Suitability**: Best for Future Appreciation. Emerging as an aerospace and industrial hub.
* **Price Range**: 3BHK: ₹65L – ₹90L; Plots: ₹35k – ₹50k/sq.yd.
* **Infrastructure**: Immediate airport proximity and the Srisailam Highway growth corridor.
* **Downsides**: Currently lacks established social infrastructure (large malls/multiplexes).
* **Choice**: Plots for massive appreciation; Apartments for those working in the nearby SEZs. 

### 5. Patancheru / Isnapur (West Outskirts)

* **Distance to Airport**: ~45–50 KM via ORR Exit 3.
* **Suitability**: Balanced (Value Entry). The most affordable entry into the Western growth story.
* **Price Range**: 3BHK: ₹55L – ₹85L; Plots: ₹30k – ₹45k/sq.yd.
* **Infrastructure**: Planned Metro Phase 2 extension from Miyapur.
* **Downsides**: Industrial history; it is critical to select projects away from older chemical zones.
* **Choice**: Apartments for low-entry residential use.
